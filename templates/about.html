Welcome to Kusina Buddy

your simple, smart, and reliable cooking companion.

Kusina Buddy was created to make everyday meal planning easier for everyone. We know how stressful it can be to think about what to cook, especially when time and ingredients are limited. That’s why we built an AI-powered tool that helps you generate recipes based on what you already have, plan meals for the week, and stay organized with built-in tools like a calculator and notepad.

Behind this project is Infinite Loop Thinkers, a group of NIT Batch 5 learners. While we come from different skill levels and backgrounds, we share a common goal: to learn, grow, and create practical, user-friendly tech solutions. Through the program, we gained skills in Python and web technologies, allowing us to combine creativity and technology to solve real problems in simple, meaningful ways.

Kusina Buddy is our step toward making cooking more enjoyable, reducing food waste, and helping home cooks—from beginners to busy parents—feel more confident in the kitchen. Thank you for using Kusina Buddy. We hope it makes your cooking experience lighter, smarter, and more fun!

Recipe Generator

A recipe generator is an AI-powered tool that uses user inputs, such as available ingredients, dietary requirements, and preferences, to create custom recipes. It then employs algorithms to produce distinctive, detailed instructions, assisting users in reducing food waste and discovering new dishes.

Calculator

The built-in calculator helps users quickly compute ingredient quantities based on the number of servings they need. It can also be used to recalculate measurements to ensure accuracy and consistency while cooking. Beyond recipes, the calculator can also assist in managing finances by estimating ingredient costs, helping users plan budgets, compare prices, and make cost-effective meal choices.

Notepad

The built-in notepad in the recipe generator allows users to write down personal notes, cooking tips, or ingredient substitutions for future reference. It helps keep ideas organized while cooking or planning meals. This feature makes the recipe generator more personalized and convenient to use.

Store Finder

The built-in store finder in the recipe generator helps users easily locate nearby grocery stores where they can buy the needed ingredients. It saves time by showing convenient options based on location and availability. This feature makes meal planning smoother by connecting recipes directly to real-world shopping needs.
